Core Business Problem

SaaS metrics are highly sensitive to logo churn and contract contraction rates. Lacking detailed, multi-stage database joins across billing records and activity logs, executive leadership is often forced to rely on delayed indicators, unable to pinpoint which customer success teams or acquisition campaigns drive solid retention.

This portfolio project addresses this visibility gap by transforming raw transactional billing records into a robust **SaaS Subscription Analytics Pipeline**. By joining active subscription databases with platform login logs and transaction tables, we calculate active MRR growth trends, logo churn rates, expansion MRR balances, and LTV-to-CAC ratios.

Immediate SaaS Interventions

  • Active Churn Mitigation (Underutilized Accounts): Database logs reveal that **10.9% of active subscribers** show zero platform activity in 30 days. Deploy automated, customer success checkins and specific feature tutorials to re-engage accounts before manual monthly cancellations occur.
  • Credit Card Decline Retry Sequence: Financial audits show that **4.2% of failed transactions** are due to credit card expiration errors on recurring Standard tier renewals. Implement an automated smart-retry sequence (e.g. retrying on paydays) and in-app card updates to prevent involuntary churn.
